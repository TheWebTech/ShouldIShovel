The goal of this app is to allow you to be as lazy as possible. You don't even need to enter your location. We just straight up tell you if you need to shovel.

This app uses your current physical location, various factors from how much snow has fallen, and the temperatures for the next few days in your area. None of your data is saved/stored or sold your general location is used to query a popular weather API anonymously. The suggestions provided by this app are not legal advice, if you are legally obligated to clear snow that is your responsibility.
